# Proof of Alpha

> Pick up girls. On-chain. On Solana.

Spend **AURA** to approach. Say the right things in 4 messages. Close or get rejected — forever recorded on Solana devnet.

---

## The Concept

You are an archetype. She is an AI with standards. You have 4 messages to impress her, then you pick your closer. Win and you earn AURA. Lose and you walk away with nothing — and she gets the last word on-chain.

Every approach costs AURA. Every win pays AURA. Every result syncs to a live leaderboard. Your wallet is your identity.

---

## How It Works

```
Connect Wallet → Pick Archetype → Enter a Venue → Approach a Girl → Chat 4 Messages → Pick Closer → Win or Lose AURA
```

### The Closer

After your 4th message you pick one:

| Closer | Risk | Reward |
|--------|------|--------|
| **Flirt** | High — she can still reject you | High payout if she's into it |
| **Flex** | Medium — safer odds, lower ceiling | Moderate payout |
| **Leave** | None — guaranteed safe exit | Half your approach cost back |

Win chance is calculated from your conversation score + your archetype stats. The AI scores every message secretly (-10 to +10). You won't see the score — just her reaction.

### Streak Multiplier

Win back-to-back and your AURA payout multiplies:

| Streak | Multiplier |
|--------|-----------|
| 1 win | 1× |
| 2 wins | 1.25× |
| 3–4 wins | 1.75× |
| 5+ wins | **3×** |

## Your Archetype

Three playable characters. Stats scale from level 1 to 10 — spend AURA to level up.

### Alpha — Charismatic Aggressor
> Born to lead. Built to dominate.

High Aggression + Greed. Charges hard, expects results. Best closer: **Flirt** and **Flex**.
Unique move: **Intimidate** — force an opponent to reveal their move before confirm.
Beats Beta. Loses to Sigma.

### Beta — Defensive Loyalist
> At least I tried.

High Defense + Bluff. The nice guy who outlasts everyone. Low offensive ceiling but hard to eliminate.
Unique move: **Apologize** — reduce the highest-balance opponent's aggression by 20% for 1 round.
Beats Alpha. Loses to Sigma.

### Sigma — Cold Assassin
> You already lost before you played.

High Bluff + Aggression. Reads patterns, exploits trust, strikes precisely.
Unique move: **Cold Read** — predict one opponent's next move with 70% accuracy before confirm.
Beats Alpha. Loses to Beta.

## Leaderboard

Live rankings via Supabase realtime. Three tabs:

- **Players** — ranked by total AURA
- **Streaks** — ranked by longest win streak
- **Gangs** — ranked by combined gang AURA

Your wallet address links to a public profile showing your stats, rank, and gang.
